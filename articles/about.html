About us

Founded in 2022 by Pranav PD, who serves as the founder and chief editor, Beyond Science was born out of a passion for showcasing the incredible work of researchers and science communicators from around the world. We noticed a gap in the media landscape—there wasn't a dedicated magazine that truly appreciated and featured the stories of researchers, the real truth seekers.

At Beyond Science, we believe that the people behind the scientific discoveries are just as important as the discoveries themselves. Our mission is to bring their stories to the forefront, highlighting their journeys, challenges, and triumphs. We aim to bridge the gap between the scientific community and the public by making complex research accessible and engaging. Through compelling interviews, in-depth articles, and unique features, we strive to present science in a way that resonates with readers of all backgrounds.

Our vision is to become one of the top magazines globally, featuring the inspiring stories of researchers and their groundbreaking work. We are committed to celebrating the diversity of thought, innovation, and dedication that drives the scientific world. By spotlighting the experiences and achievements of scientists from various fields and regions, we hope to foster a greater appreciation for the pursuit of knowledge and the individuals who make it possible.

We believe researchers are the real truth seekers, relentlessly pursuing knowledge and uncovering new insights that push the boundaries of human understanding. Our journey began with a vibrant Instagram community, where we quickly garnered support and encouragement from senior researchers who recognized the value of our mission. Their appreciation and enthusiasm have been a driving force behind our commitment to showcasing the incredible work being done in laboratories and research institutions around the globe.

Join us in our journey to shine a light on the brilliant minds shaping our future. At Beyond Science, every researcher's story is a testament to the relentless pursuit of discovery, and we are here to share these remarkable narratives with the world. Together, we can bridge the divide between science and society, inspiring curiosity and promoting a deeper understanding of the world around us. Your support and engagement are crucial as we continue to highlight the stories that matter and celebrate the true spirit of scientific inquiry.
